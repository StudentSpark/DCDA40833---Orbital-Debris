Research Question Answer

I believe that this project satisfyingly answers the research question, but also brings many more questions to the table. While for many, space is a place restricted to textbooks and television, operations in space actually play a massive and increasingly important role in daily life. As such, many seemingly distant issues in space can have very real impacts on the day-to-day lives of people everywhere. This research also brings up many potential avenues for further research and study. Research into the effectiveness and practicality of space debris removal technologies will become increasingly important as Earth’s orbit continues to be developed. Additionally, further research into orbital mechanics and astrophysics will grant a better personal understanding of the environment and how satellites operate within it. My resarch also posed the question on how to govern space on an international level. Althought there is the 1967 Treaty on Principles Governing the Activities of States in the Exploration and Use of Outer Space, including the Moon and Other Celestial Bodies, it does not provide a comprehensive framework for addressing the growing issue of space debris or international cooperation in space. Additionally there are international advisory committies such as the Inter-Agnecy Space Debris Coordination Committee (IADC) and the UN Committee on the Peaceful Uses of Outer Space (UNCOPUOUS), neither have the power to enforce compliance, only provide recommendations. While I’ve always loved space, this has been my first foray into the nitty-gritty details of it. Even so, my research stays at a relatively high-level of focus, avoiding the hard number-crunching probabilities, personal forecasts, and in-depth projections.
